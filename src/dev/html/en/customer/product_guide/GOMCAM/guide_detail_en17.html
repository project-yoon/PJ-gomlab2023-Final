Extract Audio

You can extract audio from video. Select the file you want to extract audio from and click the [Extract Audio] icon. The audio will be extracted automatically.

Extract Audio

Extract audio in the following order.

  • ① Select the video file you want to extract audio from in the explorer area and click the [Extract Audio] icon.
  • ② Click the [Extract Audio] icon, and the audio extraction list pops up on the right side of the explorer area. You can check the progress of the audio files being extracted in real-time. After the extraction is completed, you can click the [Open Folder] button to check the saved audio extract files in the folder.
