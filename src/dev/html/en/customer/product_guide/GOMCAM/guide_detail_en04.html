Recording and Capture Menu

1. Screen Recording

When clicking the [ Screen Recording] menu then specifying the area you want to record on the PC screen, the recording toolbar will be exposed. Use the recording toolbar to record your screen and take advantage of additional features when recording.

If you click the [More ] button, you can use additional recording menus.
For the most recently used recording function, a new menu button is added under the [Screen Recording] menu.
For example, if you click Scheduled Recording after clicking the [More] button, the [Scheduled Recording] menu will appear in the first order below the [Screen Recording] menu button, and you can quickly access the function for the next recording.

Screen Recording - More
Full-Screen Recording It allows you to record your entire PC screen. If you have more than 2 monitors connected, you need to select 1 monitor that you want to record on.
Webcam Recording You can record it after selecting the webcam device connected to your PC.
Lecture Recording After selecting the PowerPoint, HanShow, or PDF document you want to record, you can record it in Slide Show (full-screen) mode.
Scheduled Recording Scheduled Recording is a feature that starts recording on a specific date and time and automatically records for a set amount of time. In the Scheduled Recording pop-up, you can add or edit the recording schedule you desire.
Direct Input You can set the size of the recording area and record.
Recently Recorded Area You can re-record the recently recorded area.

2. Image Capture

After clicking the [ Image Capture] menu, specify the area you want to capture the image on the PC screen to complete the capture.

If you click the [More] button, you can use other various capture functions.
The most recently used capture function has been added as a new menu under the [Image Capture] menu button for quick access.

Image Capture - More
Full Screen Capture It allows you to capture your entire PC screen. If you have more than 2 monitors connected, you must select 1 monitor that you want to capture.
Capture Fixed Area You can use the capture toolbar to capture the desired area continuously.
Multi-monitor Capture It allows you to capture the screen of all monitors connected to your PC at once.
GIF Animation GIF animation is when you put a series of images in a single file and make it look like an animation. You can use the Capture Toolbar to create any GIF animation you want.
Auto-scroll capture If your web browser screen has a scroll bar, the entire content will be captured in a single image by automatically scrolling through the browser.
Recently Captured Area You can recapture the recently captured area.
