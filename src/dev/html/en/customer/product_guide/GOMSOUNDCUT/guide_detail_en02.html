Import Files

Importing from a PC

You can click the top folder icon and the Find File button at the center to find and retrieve files on your PC.
You can easily drag and drop sound files directly to import them.

Import From Gom Voice

In GOM Voice, click on the audio file and then click the edit icon to launch GOM Sound Cut.
The audio file will be automatically added to GOM Sound Cut.
