Simultaneous Recording

Simultaneous recording is a feature that allows you to record multiple videos or sound sources together using the ghost button.
Here's how to record at the same time.

  • 1. Check [Use Ghost Button] in Settings (F5).
  • 2. Click and drag the record button and drop it onto the Play button on the desired media source.
  • 3. Go back to Sound Recorder and click Record.
  • 4. The ghost button and record button will be pressed at the same time, and playback and recording will start at the same time.
