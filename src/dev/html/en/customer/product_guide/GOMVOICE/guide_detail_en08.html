Adjusting the Volume Mixer

You can use the volume mixer to adjust the volume of a specific piece of audio.

Here's how to adjust the volume mixer.

  • 1. Click [Settings] on the main screen.
  • 2. [Recorder tab> Open Volume Mixer..] Click
  • 3. Decrease or increase the volume of the area you want to adjust with the mouse.
